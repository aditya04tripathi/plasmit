# Improving Patient Outcomes in Critical Care

The "Golden Hour" in emergency and critical care is paramount. By digitizing care workflows, hospitals can achieve a significant reduction in mortality rates.

## The Value of Contextual Care
Contextual care means having the right data at the right time. When an intensivist can see the patient's state on a mobile device, the speed of intervention increases.

### Key Benefits:
1. **Reduction in Medical Errors**: Automated alerts for deterioration.
2. **Bed Utilisation**: Optimizing the flow of patients from ICU to HDU.
3. **Enhanced Collaboration**: Seamless communication between multidisciplinary teams.
