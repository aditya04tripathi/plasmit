# The Future of Smart ICU Monitoring

Integrating AI at the edge allows for real-time anomaly detection without the latency of cloud round-trips. 

## Key Technological Shifts
- **Edge Computing**: Moving computation closer to the patient.
- **Real-time Streaming**: Using high-throughput protocols for vitals.
- **Predictive Analytics**: Shifting from reactive to proactive care.

This approach significantly reduces the cognitive load on clinicians.
