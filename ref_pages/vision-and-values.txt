VISION & VALUES
PlasmIT is an Australia based health-tech, founded by healthcare visionaries and technology leaders who helped define the future care strategy for Australian and Indian government. Founders had key contributors in defining the future care interoperability standards – FHIR to democratize overall healthcare ecosystem.
Our Vision
Reimagine Healthcare services by revolutionizing stakeholders experience through technology and innovation.
Our Mission
Save Human Life by Empowering Care provider and Care receiver with next generation Smart technology-based innovative healthcare eco-system.
Our Values
PlasmIT’s values are based on our 6C’s maturity framework. Our framework drives our key principles of how we want to save millions of lives though our care delivery platform PiMed.

We aim to revolutionize stakeholders experience through our digital technology and innovations and deliver contextual care
