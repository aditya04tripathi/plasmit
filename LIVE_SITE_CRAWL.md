## Reusable Reconstruction Notes

- The live site is WordPress-style and content-heavy, not interaction-heavy.
- Messaging is repetitive across Home and PiMed.
- Hospital Corner is the strongest B2B business-case page.
- Investor Corner is concise and would benefit from stronger structure if redesigned.
- Leadership is a straightforward profile list with credential-heavy bios.
- Footer and contact information recur on nearly every page.
- Images exist throughout the site, but the textual structure is more important than the exact visual treatment if you are recreating it in a new design system.

## Recommended Recreation Order

1. Recreate global nav and footer
2. Recreate Home page narrative
3. Extract PiMed into a dedicated product page/section
4. Rebuild Hospital Corner as the main conversion page for hospital buyers
5. Rebuild Investor Corner with cleaner metrics and proof
6. Rebuild Vision & Values and Leadership as trust pages
7. Rebuild Contact page with unified canonical address data
