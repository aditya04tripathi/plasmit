## Design Context

### Users
- Hospital executives, clinical leaders, and ICU operations teams evaluating PiMed for adoption.
- Investors and strategic partners assessing product maturity and traction.
- Context: marketing site visits to understand capability, trustworthiness, and how to engage.

### Brand Personality
- Clinical, confident, visionary.
- Calm, credible, and systems-oriented rather than consumer-playful.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Premium medical technology with editorial minimalism.
- Dark-first experience with warm neutral base and a single non-blue accent.
- Avoid consumer-app flash, gimmicky gradients, or heavy card grids.

### Design Principles
- Lead with clarity and trust signals; reduce visual noise.
- Present data as structured rows and strips, not repeated cards.
- Motion is entry-only, respectful, and reduced-motion safe.
- Maintain WCAG AA contrast and strong focus states.
