2 · Problem Statement

Critical Care Gaps
Scenario A - Patient in ICU
Patient deteriorates (blood in NG tube, drop in BP, high heart rate) while senior specialist is physically outside the hospital - golden hour is lost.
Scenario B - Specialist Outside
Remote specialist lacks real-time access to clinical notes, vital signs, prescribed medication, clinical symptoms, and investigations - cannot act.

4 · Solution - PiMed (QLMed)

Smart ICU on Mobile™
One-line definition
An Intelligent and Integrated Care Delivery ecosystem for hospitals to deliver point-in-time ICU care via mobile devices, 24×7.
Three Core Pillars
24×7 Continuous Monitoring
Automated real-time medical device data streaming with anomaly highlights, intelligent alerts & insights
24×7 Connected Care
Point-in-time care intervention through real-time health data, platform-enabled decision and seamless collaboration
Compliant & Fault-Tolerant Platform
Secured, highly available, interoperable and compliant data capture, transfer, storage and display
Key Features
  • Smart ICU on Mobile™ - full ICU dashboard on a mobile device
  • Continuous monitoring with instant high-risk alerts
  • Early detection of ICU patient deterioration
  • Actionable guidance during critical situations
  • Data asset to enable predictive ICU care over time
  • AI-driven clinical pathways, engagements & workflow
  • Connects instantly with remote doctors for timely intervention
  • Continuous data-learning loop enabling predictive ICU intelligence
  • Gen AI–enabled triage & clinical pathways
  • Seamless instant collaboration between care teams
Architecture Stack
Hospital Side
Medical devices → AI-enabled Edge Devices → In-transit security → Cloud
Clinician Side
Digital channel (including mobile) → Care Delivery Platform → Secure Data Store → AI/ML Extension
