## 2. Core Product — PiMed (Smart ICU on Mobile)

**Full Name:** PiMed — Smart ICU on Mobile™
**Category:** Next-Generation Connected Digital Care Platform

### What is PiMed?

PiMed is described as a "Next-Gen Connected, Intelligent, Integrated, Immersive, Secured and Compliant Digital Care Platform" for hospitals. It promises to provide last-mile, affordable, world-class care.

PiMed is underpinned by blockchain technology and powered by AI/ML capabilities. It integrates with multiple medical devices connected to patients — whether in the ICU, emergency care, general ward, or at home — via proprietary AI/ML-enabled edge devices.

### Core Paradigm Shift

PiMed represents a fundamental shift in how hospital care is delivered:

- **From:** "Physical round" (doctors physically visiting patients at set intervals)
- **To:** "Continuous connected round" (doctors connected to patients 24×7 via mobile, from any location)

This enables point-in-time care intervention rather than reactive care, ultimately leading to better patient outcomes.

### What PiMed Enables

- Doctors to deliver 24×7 connected care, even when beyond the hospital or care settings
- Real-time patient data (vitals, device data) accessible on any mobile device, anytime, anywhere
- Tele-ICU capability — extending ICU-quality care remotely
- Transition of hospitals into "Smart Hospitals" by connecting all stakeholders and resources
- Care delivery from any device, for patients in ICU, emergency, general ward, or at home
- Leverages "One Global Care Network" philosophy

### Platform Architecture

PiMed is designed from the ground up as an intelligent and integrated care delivery ecosystem. Key architectural elements:

- Proprietary AI/ML-enabled edge devices for medical device integration
- Blockchain-based underpinning for data integrity and security
- Fault-tolerant, highly available infrastructure
- Compliant with healthcare data regulations
- Interoperable — built on FHIR standards

## 3. Key Platform Features

The following 11 features are listed as PiMed's key differentiating capabilities:

1. **Integrated Health Dashboard with Case Prioritisation** — Unified view of all patients with intelligent triage and prioritisation.
2. **Seamless Medical Device Data 24×7 on Mobile Device** — Continuous streaming of data from connected medical devices directly to care workers' mobile devices.
3. **Round-the-Clock Patient Care and Real-Time Health Vitals Monitoring** — 24×7 access to live patient vitals.
4. **AI/ML-Enabled Abnormality Alerts and Insights** — Automated anomaly detection with intelligent alerts to trigger timely intervention.
5. **Real-Time Interdisciplinary Collaboration** — Cross-specialty, cross-department collaboration enabled directly within the platform.
6. **Integrated and Guided Clinical Pathways** — Embedded clinical pathways to guide care decisions.
7. **Stakeholder-Centric One-Touch Care Experience and Insights** — Tailored experience for each type of stakeholder (doctor, nurse, administrator, etc.).
8. **Secured and Compliant Platform** — End-to-end security and regulatory compliance (includes blockchain layer).
9. **Seamless Transition Across Various Care Settings** — Continuity of care data and workflows across ICU, general ward, home care, and tele-ICU.
10. **Optimised Care Process and Efficient Resource Utilisation** — Automation and analytics to reduce waste and improve staff productivity.
11. **Health Wallet at Fingertips** — Patient health record access anywhere.

### Additional Platform Capabilities (from site content)

- Point-in-time care intervention through platform-enabled decision support and seamless collaboration
- Availability of information to all stakeholders — anywhere, any device, round the clock
